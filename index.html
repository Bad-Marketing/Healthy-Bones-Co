06Growth Area 3: Increase Products Per Subscriber

While Collagen drives the majority of subscriptions, adoption of additional Bone Renewal products remains relatively low, creating an opportunity to increase customer lifetime value through subscriber expansion.

Annatrol has emerged as the strongest secondary product within the portfolio and should serve as the primary expansion product for existing collagen subscribers.

Recommended Actions
1. Launch Subscriber Expansion Campaigns

Use advertising to target existing subscribers with complementary Bone Renewal products.

2. Prioritise Annatrol as the Expansion Product

Given its 60% YoY growth and emerging subscription base, Annatrol is the natural next step for existing Collagen subscribers.

07Advertising Strategy

Use PPC to increase products per subscriber by driving existing Collagen subscribers toward complementary Bone Renewal products, primarily Annatrol, while supporting the broader Bone Renewal Ritual messaging across the account.

Action 1: Build a Subscriber Audience via Amazon Marketing Cloud (AMC)
  • Use AMC to create a custom rule-based audience of existing Collagen subscribers (Campaign Manager → Measurement & Reporting → Amazon Marketing Cloud → Audiences).
  • This audience can then be applied as a bid modifier within PPC campaigns — increasing bids specifically for shoppers who are already subscribed, to surface complementary products more aggressively to a warm, high-intent group.
  • A prior attempt at a broader "frequent purchasers not on Subscribe & Save" audience did not meet Amazon's minimum audience size threshold. The subscriber-specific audience is more targeted and likely to qualify given subscriber count has grown from 3,773 to 6,281 (+66%) — should be revisited and tested with current volume.
  • If a query doesn't pass Amazon's review, it can be revised and resubmitted.
Action 2: Subscriber Retargeting Campaigns
  • Layer the AMC subscriber audience into Sponsored Display campaigns with an increased bid modifier.
  • Creative and copy should reinforce the Bone Renewal Ritual, positioning Annatrol as "the next step," not a separate purchase.
  • Use Sponsored Brands video/custom image placements to tell the "Foundation → Advanced Support" story in a single ad unit.
Action 3: Prioritise Annatrol as the Expansion Product
  • Increase Annatrol PPC budget allocation given its 60% YoY growth and emerging subscription base.
  • Test "Complete the Ritual" bundle messaging in Sponsored Brands headlines.
Action 4: Cross-Sell via Branded Defense + Expansion Combo
  • Maintain strong branded campaign coverage on Collagen (protects subscriber funnel from competitor takeover).
  • Layer in product-targeting campaigns where Annatrol, Creatine + HMB, and Electrolytes ads appear on Collagen's product page placements (PAT campaigns).

08Growth Area 4: Scale Creator Connections

Creator Connections is generating strong returns, with current campaigns delivering approximately 7–8x ROAS. Existing campaigns are heavily focused on Collagen and the 90-Day Commitment framework, which appears to be resonating with customers.

Q3 should focus on expanding beyond Collagen and leveraging creators to educate customers on the complete Bone Renewal Framework.

Action 1: Increase Creator Volume

Scale the campaigns by increasing:

  • Active creator partnerships
  • Product participation

The goal should be to significantly increase the amount of creator-generated content supporting the Healthy Bones ecosystem.

Action 2: Align Creator Campaigns with the Bone Renewal Ritual

Expand beyond collagen-only campaigns and begin building creator content around the complete Bone Renewal Ritual.

Potential Campaign Themes
  • Morning Bone Renewal Ritual
  • Foundation Ritual
  • 90-Day Commitment Journey
  • Advanced Bone Support
  • Building Stronger Bones for the Future
Action 3: Expand Product Participation

Future campaigns should increasingly incorporate:

  • Creatine + HMB
  • Electrolytes
  • Annatrol
  • OsteoFrax

…while continuing to use collagen as the primary entry point.

Action 4: Launch Sponsored Creator Content Campaigns

Leverage creator-generated content within Amazon advertising to:

  • Reinforce Bone Renewal messaging
  • Generate additional testing opportunities

Current campaigns deliver approximately 7–8x ROAS. The opportunity in Q3 is to replicate this performance across the full product portfolio — not just Collagen.
